About Research Railway Networks

I am a Master student at Sorbonne University and École Normale Supérieure (ENS) de Rennes in Computer Science, France, focusing on multi-agent systems and decision. My research integrates decision-making (aid), societal challenges, and artificial intelligence. I like to address interdisciplinary challenges with multi-agent systems, socio-economics (e.g., mechanism design, fairness, recommendation systems), learning, and game theory to explore innovative solutions. My recent interests focus on auctions, incentives and mechanisms with impact.
