About me

I am a computer science M.Sc. student at École Normale Supérieure (ENS) de Rennes, focusing on modeling, simulation, and optimization techniques. My research integrates decision-making aid, environmental sustainability, and artificial intelligence. With a passion for addressing societal challenges through technology, I leverage multi-agent systems and game theory to explore innovative solutions. My educational background includes rigorous programs at ENS Rennes and Sorbonne University.
