Part-time Student Researcher

IRISA & ENS Rennes & Thales Group, France
Supervisors: Laurent d'Orazio, Reyyan Tekin, Dimitri Tombroff

WebAssembly serverless join: A Study of its Application.
The goal was to study WebAssembly in serverless environments compared to native performance, focused on the join operator. Insights revealed potential in using WebAssembly in such environments (up to max 2x slower than native, sometimes 20% slower only) but requiring different approaches, notably by exploiting lower cold-start times.
Paper published at SSDBM 2024.
